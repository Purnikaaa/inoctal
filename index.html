Day 1: Drive from Kathmandu to Dhap (2850m)

On Day 1, you'll embark on a scenic drive from Kathmandu to Dhap (2850m), a peaceful village in the Solu region. The journey takes you along winding mountain roads, offering spectacular views of lush hills, terraced fields, and distant snow-capped peaks. As you leave the bustling city behind, the landscape transitions into a serene mountain environment, providing a welcome escape from the urban rush.

Day 2: Trek from Dhap to Jhapre (2920m)

Embark on your trek from Dhap to Jhapre, a beautiful village known for its Buddhist monasteries and panoramic views of Everest and Numbur Himal. Enjoy the serene trails and immerse yourself in the local Sherpa culture.

Day 3: Trek from Jhapre to Pikey Base Camp (3650m)

Today, trek from Jhapre to Pikey Base Camp, where you will be rewarded with stunning views of Everest, Makalu, and Kanchenjunga. The trail offers an unforgettable experience as you traverse beautiful ridges, past ancient monasteries and lush forests.

Day 4: Sunrise Hike to Pikey Peak (4065m) and Descent to Junbesi (2675m)

Wake up early for a sunrise hike to Pikey Peak. The panoramic views from the summit are extraordinary, with breathtaking views of the Everest range. Afterward, descend to the charming village of Junbesi, a peaceful Sherpa settlement surrounded by lush landscapes.

Day 5: Trek from Junbesi to Phaplu (2413m)

Your final day of trekking takes you from Junbesi to Phaplu, where you'll board a flight back to Kathmandu. Along the way, enjoy the picturesque landscape, cross rivers, and pass through pine forests. Reflect on the memorable journey as you return to the hustle and bustle of Kathmandu.
